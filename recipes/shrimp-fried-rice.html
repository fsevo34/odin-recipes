Shrimp Fried Rice

Simple yet delicious or so I've heard. (I'm allergic to shrimp). The filling satisfying fried rice paired with widely loved shrimp, a true classic to say the least. Here we'll show you exactly how to make it with the wonderful help of Tiffy Cooks. I'm using her easy to follow recipe guide for this practice project. Shrimp Fried Rice

Ingredients:

Shrimp marinade:

  • 1lb shrimp peeled and decveined
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper

Fried Rice:

  • 3-4 large eggs (beaten and seasoned with salt and pepper)
  • 4 green onion, chopped (whites and greens seperated)
  • 5 cold cooked rice
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 5 tbsp of oil
  • 1 tsp of white pepper
  • 5 tbsp of oil
  • 2.5 tbsp vegetarian oyster sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 cup of frozen vegetables

Steps:

  1. In a bowl marinate prawns with soy sauce, cornstarch, and white pepper.
  2. On medium-high heat add 2 tbsp of oil
  3. saute prawns in oil for 3-4 minutes, once cooked put to the side
  4. Add the rest of the oil, cook in white and green parts of the onion, as well as your eggs
  5. Allow the egg to sit for 30 seconds before frying for 2-3 minutes (breaking the egg apart)
  6. Add in frozen veggies and scramble for 2 minutes
  7. Turn up the heat to high, then add your cold cooked rice and prawns
  8. Season with 1 tsp of white pepper, soy sauce, vegetarian oyster sauce, and sugar. Saute for 2-3 minutes
  9. Finally add in green onion and saute for another 1-2 minutes.
