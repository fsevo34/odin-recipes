Baked Mac and Cheese

Baked mac and cheese moooooood. Who doesn't like it? Weirdos, that's who. This fantastic recipe I'll be referencing to will help show you how easy and delicious your Baked mac and cheese can truly be. Credit to AB with Smokin' and Grillin for being a fantastic reference for my practice project.

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz elbow macaroni
  • 2-4 tablespoons salted butter (add a little extra for the baking dish)
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 2 (13 oz) cans evaporated milk
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 16 oz swiss cheese shredded
  • 16 oz medium cheddar cheese shredded
  • 16 oz cream cheese
  • 3 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoons dry mustard powder
  • 1/4 teaspoons ground nutmeg
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F and grease a large baking dish with extra butter
  2. Boil elbow macaroni according to original package (stop just at slightly underdone)
  3. In a large bowl combine cream cheese and evaporated milk. Whisk until smooth creamy mixture
  4. Pour in whole milk and add/stir in salt, cajun,mustard powder, and nutmeg. Add half of the shredded swiss and cheddar cheese, stir well
  5. Whisk a beaten egg into the mixture to help bind it all together during baking
  6. In the baking dish, combine the macaroni with cheese mixture stirring gently
  7. Top the dish with the remaining shredded swiss and cheddar cheese. Add freshly ground pepper
